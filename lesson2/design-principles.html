Rule of Thirds

That Game Company

thatgamecompany.com/journey

The game Journey uses the Rule of Thirds and Golden Ratio extensively. The game developers implemented some of these designs on their website. As you can see, the lower third is dedicated to the main character of Journey, the middle third shows the gamin platforms, and the top third has the mountain.

White Space and Clean Design

Google Fi

fi.google.com

Google is known for making good use of white space and clean, simple design. Here there is a lot of literal white space to emphasize the importance of each section. I found it interesting that there was always a little bit of color in each section to draw the eye and keep you scrolling through the page. This effect is more pronounced because of all the empty space surrounding it.

Visual Hierarchy

Wix

wix.com

The Wix website uses both color and size to establish a visual hierarchy. The first thing you see is Create a Website You're Proud Of. Next they have a call to action with the Get Started button. That there is a clear order of information to tell the viewer where to start and where to go from there.
